About Me

I am a mechanical engineer specializing in mechatronic mechanism design. I enjoy working with machines and solving novel challenges. Through my work in the robotics and automation space, I have been able to create several new technologies critical to product success, and have taken a number of designs from the early concept stage all the way to becoming deployed products successfully in use at customer sites. I strive to implement first-principals design analysis to generate robust and effective mechanical designs. I take a very hands-on approach to the design iteration process and am able to very rapidly generate prototypes using traditional and digital manufacturing techniques.

When I'm not at work, I enjoy tinkering with machines, car and bicycle maintenance, and high powered model rocketry.
